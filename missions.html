Apollo 11

The first manned mission to land on the Moon, marking a historic milestone in 1969.

Voyager 1

A space probe launched in 1977, now exploring interstellar space beyond our solar system.

Hubble Space Telescope

Launched in 1990, the Hubble revolutionized astronomy with stunning images of the cosmos.

Mars Perseverance Rover

Landing on Mars in 2021, Perseverance is searching for signs of ancient life on the red planet.

James Webb Space Telescope

Deployed in 2021, the Webb Telescope is uncovering the universe's earliest galaxies.

International Space Station (ISS)

Orbiting Earth since 1998, the ISS serves as a hub for international collaboration and research.

Chandrayaan-3

India’s successful 2023 lunar mission aimed at exploring the Moon's south pole.

New Horizons

Launched in 2006, it provided the first close-up images of Pluto and ventured into the Kuiper Belt.

Artemis I

The first uncrewed test flight of NASA's Artemis program to return humans to the Moon.

Rosetta

A mission by ESA to study and land on a comet, achieving groundbreaking success in 2014.

Kepler Mission

A telescope launched to discover Earth-like planets in the habitable zone of stars.

Galileo

A spacecraft sent to study Jupiter and its moons, launched by NASA in 1989.

SpaceX Inspiration4

The first all-civilian orbital mission, showcasing the future of private space exploration.

Pioneer 10

The first spacecraft to traverse the asteroid belt and make close observations of Jupiter.

Curiosity Rover

Exploring Mars since 2012, Curiosity has provided vital data about the planet’s habitability.

ExoMars

A European-Russian mission aimed at finding signs of life on Mars and studying its atmosphere.

Sputnik 1

The world’s first artificial satellite, launched by the Soviet Union in 1957.

Lunar Gateway

A planned space station orbiting the Moon, part of NASA’s Artemis program.

Parker Solar Probe

A mission to study the Sun’s corona and solar winds by flying closer than ever before.

Hayabusa2

A Japanese mission that returned samples from asteroid Ryugu in 2020.

Starship Test Flight

SpaceX’s ambitious program for reusable interplanetary spacecraft capable of landing on Mars.

Europa Clipper

A future NASA mission to study Jupiter’s moon Europa, which may harbor a subsurface ocean.

TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite)

Launched in 2018, TESS is designed to search for exoplanets orbiting bright stars.

Dragonfly

A planned NASA mission to explore the surface of Saturn’s moon Titan with a drone-like rotorcraft.
